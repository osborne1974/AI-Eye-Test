Eye Dominance Tester

How to Test:

  1. Hold your phone/tablet at arm's length
  2. Cover your left eye with your hand
  3. Use your finger to drag the blue dot until it's centered in the white circle
  4. Now cover your right eye
  5. If the dot stays centered, your right eye is dominant
  6. If it moves, your left eye is dominant
